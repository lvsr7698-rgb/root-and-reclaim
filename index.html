Industrial Threat Report (1990-2026)

Industrial Impact Report (1990–2026)

Executive Summary: Since 1990, industrial activities have been the primary drivers of environmental degradation. As of 2026, energy-related emissions remain at record highs, and the "nature crisis" threatens 60% of global Indigenous lands.

Top Threat Industries

  • Energy: Contributes 75.7% of global GHG emissions.
  • Agriculture: Uses 70% of freshwater; accounts for 26% of emissions.
  • Manufacturing: Cement and steel alone contribute ~15% of global CO2.
  • Fashion: Second largest water consumer; generates 20% of global wastewater.

Key Environmental Metrics

Metric Impact Detail
Biodiversity Loss Wildlife populations down ~73% since 1970.
GHG Increase CO2 emissions up 50% since 1990.
Land Degradation Affects 40% of the global population.

2026 Outlook

The "green energy transition" is creating new pressures, with mining for battery minerals now encroaching on previously protected habitats.
