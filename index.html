The Digital Footprint

The internet itself consumes massive energy. Data centers globally account for 2% of the world's electricity. While we use the web to solve problems, the web's physical existence (heavy buildings, underground cables) contributes to the Earth's burden.

SRK Research: We must "Green" our digital behavior as much as our physical behavior.

Heavy Threats: Nuclear & War

Nuclear weapons remain the biggest threat to the Earth's crust. One strike creates dead zones for centuries. Daily LPG and Oil use act as a slow-motion bomb, heating the air until the seasons fail.

Urban Weight & Infrastructure

The heavy buildings we invent sink the ground beneath them. Mobile networks interfere with the internal compass of migratory birds. Human selfishness has turned life into a "factory product."

Prevention Steps

1. Decarbonize: Replace LPG/Oil with Hydrogen/Solar.
2. Circularity: Design products for 100% recycling.
3. River Laws: Give rivers the right to sue polluters.
